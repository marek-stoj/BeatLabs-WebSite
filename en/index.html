Overview

Beat Labs is an open sandbox for rhythm games in Virtual and Mixed Reality (VR/XR). It’s powered by a database of over 70 000 community-made song beatmaps for Beat Saber. Free-to-play 😊

Community

We’re doing it in the open so come join the discussion on our Discord, Facebook or other platforms (more links below)! Please also consider supporting the development of this project via Patreon or PayPal.
